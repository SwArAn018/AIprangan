What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think like humans and mimic their actions. These machines can learn from experience, adjust to new inputs, and perform tasks that typically require human intelligence.

Usages of AI

  • Text generation and processing
  • Image generation and processing
  • Voice and audio processing
  • Video generation and processing
  • Data analysis and visualization
  • Personal assistants
